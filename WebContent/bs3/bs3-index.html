    Spanning the Skills

    Developing Web Applications is requiring a forever increasing number of almost unrelated skills. The solution is not to try to find teams Marvins.

    Multi-discipline guys are hard to find , expensive , and since they lack guru skills in any discipline, the output of such teams is often mediocrity .

    At Twist, we made it our mission to find ways to work without Marvins. Below is our tried and proven formula for faster development times, higher quality, reduced costs and far less pain...
